Effect of Cichorium Intybus L. Leaf Extract on Blood Profile, Gut Microbiota and Histopathology of Intestine of Male Rabbit

MPhil Thesis Lead Project Status:Published

Research Overview

This research was conducted to examine the impact of alcoholic extract of Cichorium intybus L. leaves on blood profile, gut microbiota and histopathology of the intestine of male rabbits.

Methodology

To undertake this study, 48 male rabbits were taken and divided into 4 groups. Varying doses of ethanolic leaf extract of Chicory were given to the experimental groups for 45 days. On 15th, 30th, and, 45th day of experiment CBC tests were run on the selected specimens from each group to investigate the hematological parameters. To evaluate the colony count of E.coli and Lactobacillus the digesta from the intestine of rabbits was removed and Plate Agar Count (PAC) test was performed. Histomorphometry was done using microtomy.

Key Findings

  • According to the Analysis of Variance the maximum physiological improvements were found in the highest dose group.
  • Furthermore, it was proved that the chicory leaf extract induced substantial improvements in all studied parameters.
